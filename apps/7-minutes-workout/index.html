Audio & visual cues

Each exercises are voice prompted. You can see and hear the timer while exercising, with a distinctive "ding" when time is up !

Customization

The application can be adapted for every sportivity levels. You can choose which exercises you want to have in your personnalized program and even the timings !

Statistics

Have a quick look a the numbers of sessions you finished so far, and try to have the best sportivity indicator, it can be over 100%!
